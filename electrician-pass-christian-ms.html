Licensed Electrical Services in Pass Christian, Mississippi

Arumi Electrical Services is a MSBOC triple-licensed electrical contractor serving Pass Christian, MS. Known as "The Pass," this historic beachfront community on the Mississippi Gulf Coast has a rich architectural heritage and a growing number of new homes — all requiring professional electrical services.

Pass Christian was devastated by Hurricane Katrina in 2005, and many homes have been rebuilt or are still undergoing renovation. Whether you're restoring a historic home, building new, or need emergency storm damage repair, Arumi provides expert electrical service tailored to Pass Christian's unique coastal environment.
