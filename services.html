Our Services

Residential Electrical

From simple outlet installations to complete home rewires, we handle every residential electrical need with precision and care. All work meets or exceeds NEC code requirements.

  • Whole house rewiring
  • Outlet and switch installation
  • Ceiling fan installation
  • Recessed and LED lighting
  • GFCI / AFCI protection
  • Smoke and CO detector installation
  • EV charger installation (Level 2)
  • Generator installation and transfer switches
  • Whole-house surge protection
  • Knob-and-tube replacement
  • Aluminum wiring remediation
