About Arumi

Precision work, rooted in craftsmanship

Our Story

Arumi LLC was founded by Sumire Maeda in Biloxi, Mississippi, with a clear mission: deliver honest, high-quality contracting work at fair prices. The name "Arumi" reflects a commitment to illumination — bringing light, power, and safety to homes and businesses across the Gulf Coast.

Based in Biloxi and serving the entire Mississippi Gulf Coast, we bring a meticulous, detail-oriented approach to every project. Whether it's a straightforward outlet installation or a complete commercial buildout, every job receives the same level of care and precision.

Meet the CEO

Sumire Maeda is the CEO and Manager of Arumi LLC. She holds Mississippi State Board of Contractors (MSBOC) licenses for electrical, mechanical, and plumbing — a rare triple-licensed contractor capable of handling multi-trade projects under one roof. Arumi LLC is incorporated in Mississippi (Business ID: 1498085) and has been in good standing since July 2025.

Our Approach

We believe every project should be done right the first time. That means:

  • Clean, code-compliant installations
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden costs
  • Clear communication throughout every project
  • Respect for your property and your time
  • Standing behind our work with solid warranties

Licensed & Insured

Arumi LLC is fully licensed by the Mississippi State Board of Contractors for electrical, mechanical, and plumbing work. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and pull all required permits. Every installation meets or exceeds the National Electrical Code (NEC), International Mechanical Code (IMC), and local requirements.

Service Area

We proudly serve the Mississippi Gulf Coast, including Biloxi, Gulfport, Ocean Springs, D'Iberville, Long Beach, Pass Christian, Bay St. Louis, Gautier, and Pascagoula. If you're on the coast, we've got you covered.
