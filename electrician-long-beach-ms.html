Licensed Electrical Services in Long Beach, Mississippi

Arumi Electrical Services provides professional, code-compliant electrical work for homes and businesses in Long Beach, MS. Located between Gulfport and Pass Christian along the beautiful Mississippi Gulf Coast, Long Beach is a charming beachfront community with a mix of historic homes and newer developments — all of which need reliable electrical service.

Many Long Beach homes, especially those near the beach, face unique electrical challenges including salt air corrosion, hurricane damage repair, and the need for generator systems. Arumi specializes in these Gulf Coast-specific electrical needs, delivering work that's built to withstand coastal conditions.
