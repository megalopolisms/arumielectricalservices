Licensed Electrical Services in Bay St. Louis, Mississippi

Arumi Electrical Services provides professional electrical work for homes and businesses in Bay St. Louis, MS and throughout Hancock County. Known as "The Bay," this artistic coastal community on the Mississippi Gulf Coast features a thriving downtown, historic homes, and growing residential neighborhoods — all requiring dependable electrical service from a licensed contractor.

Bay St. Louis and neighboring Waveland continue to rebuild and grow since Hurricane Katrina. Many homes need electrical upgrades, panel replacements, and storm-hardening improvements. Arumi brings MSBOC triple-licensed expertise to every Bay St. Louis project, ensuring all work meets current code requirements and is built to withstand Gulf Coast weather.
