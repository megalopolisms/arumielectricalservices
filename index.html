Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our electrical services

What areas does Arumi Electrical serve?

Arumi serves the entire Mississippi Gulf Coast, including Biloxi, Gulfport, Ocean Springs, D'Iberville, Long Beach, Pass Christian, Bay St. Louis, Gautier, Pascagoula, and Moss Point. We handle both residential and commercial electrical projects throughout Harrison, Jackson, and Hancock counties.

Is Arumi licensed and insured?

Yes. Arumi LLC holds MSBOC (Mississippi State Board of Contractors) licenses for electrical, mechanical, and plumbing — making us a rare triple-licensed contractor. We are fully insured with comprehensive liability coverage, and we pull all required permits for every job.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all residential and commercial projects. Call us at (305) 497-9133 or fill out our online contact form to schedule your free estimate.

Do you handle emergency electrical repairs?

Yes. We offer 24/7 emergency electrical service for urgent safety situations including power outages, exposed wiring hazards, breaker failures, and storm damage. Call (305) 497-9133 for immediate assistance.

Can you install an EV charger at my home?

Absolutely. We install Level 2 EV charging stations including Tesla Wall Connector, ChargePoint, and universal NEMA 14-50 outlets. This includes panel assessment, dedicated circuit installation, and all required permitting.

What types of panel upgrades do you perform?

We perform 100A, 150A, and 200A panel upgrades, subpanel installations, meter base replacements, and full service entrance upgrades. All work meets or exceeds current NEC code requirements and includes proper permitting and inspection.
